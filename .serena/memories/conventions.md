# Conventions
- Keep buildless/static unless scope materially requires a framework.
- English is the default public language; Korean toggle supports local buddies and stakeholders.
- CTA URLs appear in hardcoded anchors and inline `CONFIG`; keep both and visible labels aligned.
- `CONTENT_MAP` owns bilingual visible copy and dynamic metadata; initial fallback DOM metadata must match English entries.
- Do not invent unconfirmed price, venue, capacity, time, schedule mapping, payment, inclusions, cancellation, refund, or guarantees.
- Deploy only approved WebP derivatives with meaningful alt text; never deploy raw JPG/JPEG or EXIF-bearing originals.
- Keep maintainer-only validation details and private participant information out of all public surfaces.
